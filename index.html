Digital Twin You - Samsung PRISM GenAI Hackathon 2025
Project Overview

Digital Twin You is an AI-powered camera assistant that learns your personal photography patterns and proactively suggests optimal camera settings. Built specifically for the Samsung Galaxy ecosystem, it creates a "digital twin" of your photography behavior to deliver personalized, intelligent camera experiences.

Problem Statement

Users spend 2+ minutes manually adjusting camera settings for routine photos. Generic camera AI doesn't learn individual user preferences, and repetitive daily photography tasks lack personalization.

Key Features
  • Behavioral Pattern Recognition
  • Intelligent Setting Prediction
  • Privacy-First Architecture with Samsung Knox

Google Maps Scraper
Project Overview

A powerful Chrome extension designed to effortlessly scrape business data from Google Maps search results. Extract key information such as business names, ratings, reviews, addresses, phone numbers, websites, and operating hours with just a few clicks. Perfect for market research, lead generation, and data analysis.

Key Features
  • Easy Scraping: One-click scraping from Google Maps search pages.
  • Comprehensive Data Extraction: Captures title, rating, review count, address, phone number, company website, and timings.
  • Infinite Scroll Support: Automatically scrolls through results to collect all available data.
  • CSV Export: Download scraped data as a CSV file for easy analysis.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive popup interface with real-time feedback.
  • Smart Data Parsing: Advanced algorithms to accurately identify and extract business information.
  • Email Notifications (Optional): Backend service for sending email notifications with scraped data.
